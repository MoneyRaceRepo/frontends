# AI Strategy Assistant

## Overview

Money Race features an intelligent AI assistant that analyzes your profile and recommends the optimal savings strategy.

---

## How It Works

### Input Analysis

The AI considers:

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Risk Tolerance | 30% | Your comfort with volatility |
| Financial Goals | 25% | Short-term vs long-term |
| Savings History | 20% | Past consistency patterns |
| Market Conditions | 15% | Current market trends |
| Time Horizon | 10% | Challenge duration |

---

### Strategy Output

Based on analysis, the AI recommends one of three strategies:

---

## Conservative Strategy

**Risk Level:** Low  
**Best For:** Risk-averse savers, beginners

**Characteristics:**
- Stable, predictable growth
- Lower potential returns
- Maximum capital protection
- Ideal for emergency funds

**Typical Allocation:**
```
├── 80% Stable assets
├── 15% Low-risk yield
└── 5%  Buffer
```

---

## Balanced Strategy

**Risk Level:** Medium  
**Best For:** Moderate savers, most users

**Characteristics:**
- Mix of stability and growth
- Moderate returns potential
- Good risk-reward balance
- Recommended for 8-week challenges

**Typical Allocation:**
```
├── 50% Stable assets
├── 35% Growth yield
└── 15% Performance buffer
```

---

## Aggressive Strategy

**Risk Level:** High  
**Best For:** Experienced savers, high risk tolerance

**Characteristics:**
- Higher growth potential
- Increased volatility exposure
- Maximum reward opportunity
- Best for long-term rooms

**Typical Allocation:**
```
├── 30% Stable assets
├── 50% Growth yield
└── 20% High-yield opportunities
```

---

## Strategy Comparison

| Aspect | Conservative | Balanced | Aggressive |
|--------|--------------|----------|------------|
| Risk | Low | Medium | High |
| Potential Return | Low | Medium | High |
| Volatility | Very Low | Moderate | High |
| Best Duration | 4 weeks | 8 weeks | 12 weeks |

---

## Changing Strategy

You can change your strategy once per challenge:

1. Go to Room → Settings
2. Select "Change Strategy"
3. Review AI recommendation
4. Confirm new strategy

**Note:** Strategy changes take effect from the next week.
