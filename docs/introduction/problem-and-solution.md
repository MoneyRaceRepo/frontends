# Problem and Solution

## ‚ùå The Problem

### 1. Lack of Savings Discipline

> **70% of people struggle to maintain consistent savings habits.**

Traditional savings methods offer no accountability, no motivation, and no consequences for missing deposits. People start with good intentions but quickly lose momentum.

### 2. No Social Support

Saving money is typically a **solitary activity**. Without community support or peer accountability, it's easy to give up when motivation fades.

### 3. Boring Experience

Traditional savings methods lack gamification, rewards, and sense of progress. Users need more than just a savings account.

### 4. Information Overload

With countless investment options available, users often suffer from **analysis paralysis**. They don't know which strategy suits their risk tolerance and goals.

### 5. Trust Issues in DeFi

Many DeFi savings protocols are:
- Too complex for beginners
- Lack transparency
- Have high entry barriers
- Subject to smart contract risks
