# Glossary

## A

### AI Strategy
The intelligent recommendation system that suggests Conservative, Balanced, or Aggressive savings strategies based on user profile.

---

## C

### Consistency Score
A metric measuring how regularly a participant makes their weekly deposits. Higher scores earn more rewards.

### Conservative Strategy
Low-risk savings approach with stable returns and maximum capital protection.

---

## D

### Deposit Window
The weekly timeframe during which participants must make their deposits (typically 7 days).

---

## G

### Gas Fee
Small transaction fee paid in SUI tokens to execute blockchain operations.

---

## M

### Move
The smart contract programming language used on Sui blockchain.

---

## P

### Penalty
Fee charged when a participant misses their weekly deposit. Goes into the reward pool.

### Protocol Fee
A 5% fee taken from rewards to sustain the Money Race protocol.

---

## R

### Reward Pool
The collection of penalty fees that are distributed to consistent savers at challenge end.

### Room
A savings challenge group where participants commit to weekly deposits.

---

## S

### Streak
The number of consecutive weeks a participant has made on-time deposits.

### Sui
The Layer 1 blockchain that Money Race is built on, known for fast transactions and low fees.

---

## T

### TVL (Total Value Locked)
The total amount of funds deposited across all Money Race rooms.

---

## U

### USDC
USD Coin, a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. The primary token for Money Race deposits.

---

## W

### Weekly Target
The minimum amount a participant must deposit each week to stay consistent.

---

## Z

### zkLogin
Sui's zero-knowledge authentication that allows users to sign in with web2 accounts (Google, Apple) without a crypto wallet.
