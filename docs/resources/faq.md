# FAQ

## General Questions

### What is Money Race?

Money Race is a gamified group savings protocol on Sui blockchain where users join savings rooms, make weekly deposits, and earn rewards for staying consistent.

---

### How do I get started?

1. Visit the Money Race app
2. Connect with Google (zkLogin) or Sui wallet
3. Browse and join a savings room
4. Make your first deposit

---

### Is my money safe?

Yes. All funds are secured by audited smart contracts on Sui blockchain. The protocol is non-custodial – you always control your funds.

---

### What token do I need?

You need **USDC** for deposits and a small amount of **SUI** for transaction fees.

---

## Savings Rooms

### How do I join a room?

Click on any room from the dashboard, review the parameters, and click "Join". You'll need to make an initial deposit to participate.

---

### Can I create my own room?

Yes. Go to Dashboard → "Create Room" and set your preferred parameters (duration, weekly target, etc.).

---

### What happens if I miss a deposit?

A penalty fee (typically 5-20%) is deducted from your deposit balance and added to the reward pool for consistent savers.

---

### Can I leave a room early?

You can leave before the challenge starts. Once it begins, you're committed for the duration.

---

## Rewards

### How are rewards calculated?

Rewards come from penalties paid by participants who miss deposits. They're distributed proportionally based on your consistency score.

---

### When do I receive rewards?

Rewards are distributed automatically at the end of the challenge. You can withdraw them immediately.

---

### Are there any fees?

A 5% protocol fee is taken from the reward pool. There are no fees on your deposits.

---

## Technical

### Which wallets are supported?

Any Sui-compatible wallet including:
- Sui Wallet
- Ethos Wallet
- Suiet
- Martian

---

### What is zkLogin?

zkLogin is Sui's technology that lets you sign in with Google/Apple without needing a crypto wallet. Your keys are generated automatically.

---

### Is the code open source?

Yes. Our smart contracts and frontend are open source. Check our GitHub.
